# 🍽️ Swiggy Business Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Swiggy Business Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project that analyzes restaurant performance, customer behavior, food categories, ratings, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard converts raw restaurant data into meaningful business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze restaurant distribution across cities.
- Identify top-performing restaurants.
- Monitor customer ratings and satisfaction.
- Compare delivery performance across cities.
- Understand food category preferences.
- Enable better business decisions through analytics.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- 📊 Power BI
- 🗄️ MySQL
- 💻 SQL
- 📈 DAX
- 🔄 Power Query
- 📑 Microsoft Excel

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Restaurant Name
- City
- Food Type
- Customer Count
- Delivery Time
- Rating
- Order Details
